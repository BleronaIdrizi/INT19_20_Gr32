What is EDUCATION ?

Education is the wise, hopeful and respectful cultivation of learning undertaken in the belief that all should have the chance to share in life.Education is the process of facilitating learning, or the acquisition of knowledge, skills, values, beliefs, and habits. Educational methods include teaching, training, storytelling, discussion and directed research. Education frequently takes place under the guidance of educators, however learners can also educate themselves.
